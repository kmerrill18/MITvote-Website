Absentee Voting

COVID-19 UPDATE: If you requested an absentee ballot but you will now be home to vote, you may need to contact your board of elections to update that information. Email vote-exec@mit.edu for assistance.

MITvote is always happy to help you through the specific absentee voting process for your state! Be on the lookout for absentee ballot office hours, or feel free to email vote-exec@mit.edu with any questions!

Click the button below for more information on elections in other states.

Stamp Squad

MITvote provides free envelopes and stamps to students that need them to send in election-related materials. Email stampsquad@mit.edu to ask for envelopes or stamps, and we will meet you somewhere on campus to hand it off!

Feel free to email us with any questions you have about voting, getting involved, or anything else. We have weekly meetings, which you are welcome to attend.

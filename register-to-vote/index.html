Register to Vote!

Register in Massachusetts

If you are not an MIT student or you would rather not use TurboVote, you can register to vote in Massachusetts using the MA Online Voter Registration System. Note that if you do not have a Massachusetts state ID, you can still register in MA, but you will be redirected to a mail in form to fill out. See this document to make sure you don't miss key steps! You will need to print this form and mail it or email it in. Please either mail in the form yourself, drop the form off at an MIT Library, or see an MITvote representative. If you need an evelope and a stamp, please contact us at stampsquad@mit.edu at any time!

Register Out of State - TurboVote

If you are an MIT student, you can use TurboVote to register to vote. Click the button below to be redirected to TurboVote. MIT uses TurboVote to make the process of registering to vote easier and more straightforward for students. Check out our short TurboVote guide to avoid common mistakes and make sure you get registered!

Other Options

Otherwise, you can always Google "register to vote" + your state to find voter registration information. If you would like any help with the voter registration process, find an MITvote liaison in your living group or department, or feel free to email vote-exec@mit.edu with questions any time.

Feel free to email us with any questions you have about voting, getting involved, or anything else. We have weekly meetings, which you are welcome to attend.

Register to Vote!

Register in Massachusetts

If you are not an MIT student or you would rather not use TurboVote, you can register to vote in Massachusetts using the MA Online Voter Registration System. Note that if you do not have a Massachusetts state ID, you will be redirected to a mail in form to fill out. You will need to print this form and mail it. In fall, we will coordinate with MIT Libraries so you can turn in your forms there. However, MIT Libraries is not accepting forms at this time. Please either mail in the form yourself, or see an MITvote representative. If you need an evelope and a stamp, please contact us at stampsquad@mit.edu at any time!

Register Out of State - TurboVote

If you are an MIT student, you can use TurboVote to register to vote. Click the button below to be redirected to TurboVote. MIT uses TurboVote to make the process of registering to vote easier and more straightforward for students.

Other Options

Otherwise, you can always Google "register to vote" + your state to find voter registration information. If you would like any help with the voter registration process, find an MITvote liaison in your living group or department, or feel free to email vote-exec@mit.edu with questions any time.

Feel free to email us with any questions you have about voting, getting involved, or anything else. We have weekly meetings, which you are welcome to attend.

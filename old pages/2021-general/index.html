2021 Local Elections

The 2021 Election will take place on November 2nd, 2021!

Voting in MA

The expansion of vote-by-mail in Massachusetts seen in the 2020 Elections expired on June 30th, 2021. Thus, no-excuse vote-by-mail is not availabe for the Cambridge November 2nd Election as-of the updating of this page (July 17th).

Dates and Deadlines

  • Register to vote - received by October 13th at 8pm
  • Early voting - TBD

Early Voting

Early voting dates and times have not yet been posted for Cambridge as of the updating of this page (July 17th).

Voting in Person on Election Day

If you plan to vote in person in MA on election day, you can find your polling place using this website. If you are registered at a dorm other than Random, your polling place is probably Kresge, but you should verify that. Polls are open from 7am to 8pm. Contanct vote-exec@mit.edu with any questions.

Voter Information

2022 Midterm Elections

The 2021 Election will take place on November 28th, 2022!

What are midterm elections?

The midterm elections take place when there are federal elections but no presidential race. They take place halfway through a president's four-year term. Positions on the ballet include all members of the House of Representatives, one third of Senators, and many state level positions. In Massachusetts, state-level officials on the ballot include the governor, Secretary of State, Attorny General, state representatives, and state senators.

The midterm elections include a primary election (date varies by state) and a general election. The primary election in Massachusetts takes place on September 6th, 20220

Voting in MA

The following information applies to the general election taking place on November 8th, 2022

Dates and Deadlines

  • Register to vote - received by October 29th at 8pm, or postmarked by October 29th
  • Request a mail-in ballot - November 1st
  • Early voting - October 22nd to November 4th

Vote by Mail

Anyone can vote by mail with no excuse in Massachusetts. In order to do so, you must request a ballot by 5pm on November 1st. You can request your ballot using the online portal HERE

You must then return your ballot by 8pm on Election Day. Leave time for your ballot to travel back by mail! We recommend mailing your ballot 1 week before Election Day in order to allow enough time. You can also bring your mail-in ballot to a polling place on Election Day if you do not get it in the mail in time.

Early Voting

Early voting will take place from October 22nd to November 4th. Times and locations are not yet posted at the time of this updating (September 2nd). You can find more information about early voting in Cambridge HERE.

Voting in Person on Election Day

If you plan to vote in person in MA on election day, you can find your polling place using this website. If you are registered at a dorm other than Random, your polling place is probably Kresge, but you should verify that. Polls are open from 7am to 8pm. Contanct vote-exec@mit.edu with any questions.
